About Gaur Legal Services

Trusted legal advisors since 2005, delivering exceptional results through expertise and dedication.

Rajiv Gaur

Founder & Senior Partner

Our Founder

Rajiv Gaur established Gaur Legal Services in 2005 with a vision to create a law firm that combines legal excellence with client-centric values. With over 25 years of experience in corporate and litigation law, Mr. Gaur has been instrumental in shaping the legal landscape of our region.

A graduate of National Law School of India University, he began his career as a litigator before transitioning to corporate law. His unique perspective from both sides of the legal profession informs our firm's comprehensive approach to client service.

Under his leadership, Gaur Legal Services has grown from a small practice to one of the most respected full-service law firms in the country, recognized for its integrity, innovation, and results.

"Our success is measured by our clients' success. We don't just practice law; we build relationships and deliver solutions."

Who We Are

Gaur Legal Services is a full-service law firm with offices in eight major cities across India. Our team of over 50 dedicated legal professionals brings diverse expertise to every case we handle.

We pride ourselves on being more than just legal advisors. We are strategic partners to our clients, whether they are multinational corporations, small businesses, or individuals seeking justice.

Our firm's culture is built on collaboration, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to ethical practice. We believe that the best legal solutions come from understanding our clients' unique needs and goals.

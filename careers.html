Legal Intern

Internship
New Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore
Full-time (3-6 months)
Applications open until: 30 November 2023

Position Overview

We offer a dynamic internship program for law students looking to gain practical experience in a professional legal environment. Interns work closely with our attorneys on real cases and legal matters.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct legal research and analysis on various cases
  • Assist in drafting legal documents, contracts, and pleadings
  • Attend client meetings and court proceedings with senior lawyers
  • Help prepare case strategies and legal opinions
  • Maintain and organize case files and legal documentation
  • Participate in firm meetings and training sessions

Requirements

  • Currently enrolled in an LLB program (3rd year or above) from a recognized university
  • Strong academic record with demonstrated interest in legal practice
  • Excellent research, writing, and analytical skills
  • Proficiency in legal research tools and Microsoft Office
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • Professional demeanor and strong ethical standards

What We Offer

  • Hands-on experience with real cases and clients
  • Mentorship from experienced attorneys
  • Stipend for full-time interns
  • Potential for future employment opportunities
  • Professional development workshops

Associate Lawyer

Full-time
New Delhi
Full-time
Applications open until: 15 December 2023

Position Overview

We are seeking a motivated Associate Lawyer with 2-3 years of experience to join our corporate law practice. The ideal candidate will have strong analytical skills and a passion for legal excellence.

Key Responsibilities

  • Handle a diverse caseload of corporate legal matters
  • Draft and review contracts, agreements, and legal documents
  • Represent clients in negotiations and legal proceedings
  • Conduct thorough legal research and analysis
  • Collaborate with senior partners on complex cases
  • Maintain client relationships and provide legal counsel

Requirements

  • LLB degree from a recognized university
  • Enrolled with the Bar Council of India
  • 2-3 years of relevant legal experience
  • Strong knowledge of corporate law and procedures
  • Excellent communication and advocacy skills
  • Ability to manage multiple priorities and deadlines

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and benefits package
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Collaborative work environment
  • Exposure to high-profile cases and clients
  • Clear path for career advancement
